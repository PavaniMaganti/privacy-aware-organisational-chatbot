Northbridge University Approved Course Information

MSc Artificial Intelligence
Duration: One year full-time.
Start date: September.
Entry requirements: A UK second-class honours degree or an international
equivalent in computing, engineering, mathematics, science or another
relevant subject.
English-language requirement: IELTS 6.5 overall, with no individual
component below 6.0.

MSc Data Science
Duration: One year full-time.
Start date: September.
Entry requirements: A relevant undergraduate degree in computing,
mathematics, engineering, science or a related subject.
English-language requirement: IELTS 6.5 overall, with no individual
component below 6.0.

MSc Business Analytics
Duration: One year full-time.
