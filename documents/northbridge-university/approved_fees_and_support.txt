Northbridge University Approved Fees and Support Information

MSc Artificial Intelligence
UK tuition fee: £12,500.
International tuition fee: £18,750.

MSc Data Science
UK tuition fee: £12,000.
International tuition fee: £18,000.

MSc Cyber Security
UK tuition fee: £12,250.
International tuition fee: £18,250.

MSc Business Analytics
UK tuition fee: £11,750.
International tuition fee: £17,500.

Students may pay tuition fees in three instalments. The first instalment
must be paid before enrolment.

The International Excellence Scholarship is worth £3,000. International
applicants are automatically considered after receiving an academic offer.

Student accommodation applications open in April.

A full tuition-fee refund may be available when a student formally
withdraws before enrolment. Refund requests submitted after enrolment are
assessed under the university refund policy.
