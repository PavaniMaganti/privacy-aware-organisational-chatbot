NovaTech Solutions Approved Product Information

NovaHub Mini
The NovaHub Mini is a smart-home controller.
It connects through Wi-Fi.
It supports Android 11 or later and iOS 16 or later.
Initial setup normally takes five minutes.

To set up the NovaHub Mini:
1. Connect the device to power.
2. Open the NovaTech mobile application.
3. Select Add New Device.
4. Follow the instructions shown in the application.

NovaCam Pro
The NovaCam Pro is an indoor security camera.
It connects through Wi-Fi.
It supports Android 11 or later and iOS 16 or later.
Initial setup normally takes approximately ten minutes.
